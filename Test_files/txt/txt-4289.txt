Short Narrative
The Pak-Benelux Overseas Chamber of Commerce and Industry is an effort to strengthen and diversify trade
relations between Pakistan and Benelux countries (Belgium, Netherlands, Luxembourg. The forum shall
contribute in enhancing interactions between industrialists, traders, researchers and governmental officials. The
forum shall act as central point to disseminate information, background researches and product promotions. The
forum shall facilitate traders and promoters of both Pakistan and Benelux countries. Besides that, the forum shall
enhance social, educational and cultural interactions.
 
Short Narrative
The newly elected government of Pakistan reflects the ambitions and aspirations of the people of Pakistan for a
changed Pakistan. The vision of the new government and its visionary leader  is centered on a dignified nation
with fully realized economic potential exchanged with community of nations. The practical implementation of such
a grand vision needs focused interventions from individuals and collectives.
The PABOCCI envisions a facilitating role between the governments and individual traders/industrialists. There is
a lot of space to enhance the trade potential. Belgium is the 10th largest trading partner of Pakistan with total
trade amounting to $700.7 million. Pakistan`s major exports to Belgium include cotton fabrics, synthetic textiles,
readymade garments, hosiery, bed ware, fish and fish preparations, sports goods and leather clothing. The
Belgian exports to Pakistan include chemical industry products, metals, plant and machinery, plastic and rubber
products.
Similarly, Netherland is the 8th largest trading partner of Pakistan with trade magnitude of $ 758 million.
Pakistan`s imports from Netherlands include mineral fuels foils, distillation products, cereals, flour, starch, milk
products, machinery, boilers, pharmaceutical products, plastics, organic chemicals, iron and steel. The Pakistan´s
exports to Netherlands include articles of apparel, cotton, beverages, spirits, vinegar, articles of leather, copper,
sports requisites, and sugar, among others.

Mission Statement
PABOCCI shall strive to enhance business and trade relations between Pakistan and Benelux countries through
facilitating and providing relevant information and background knowledge to interested trade partners. PABOCCI
shall also facilitate technology and culture exchanges. 
 
Objectives
To contribute towards enhance trade relations between Pakistan and Benelux countries
To help transfer of technology from Benelux countries to Pakistan
To help Pakistan overcoming environmental challenges
To impart knowledge about European experimentation regarding agriculture sector
To facilitate cultural interactions between Pakistan and Benelux countries
